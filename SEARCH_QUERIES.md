# Search Query Specifications

This document provides the exact Meta Content Library (MCL) search queries used in the study.

---

### Search 1: Field Mapping (Core Aggregated Dataset)

**Query:**
```
#CorporateTax | #GlobalTax | #FairTax | #TaxJustice | #BEPS | #PillarTwo | 
#PillarOne | #G7Tax | #G20Tax | #GlobalMinimumTax | #OECDTax | #GlobalTaxDeal | 
#MinimumTax | #TaxHavens
```

**Parameters:**
- Time Range: January 1, 2013 – December 31, 2025
- Language: English
- Posts retrieved: 13,880

**Purpose:** Provide a longitudinal baseline of global tax reform narratives across all major reforms to compare volume and visibility trends.

---

### Search 2: BEPS 1.0 (Initial Reform Cycle)

**Query:**
```
#BEPS | #TaxAvoidance | #TransferPricing | #BaseErosion | #ProfitShifting | #TaxHaven
```

**Parameters:**
- Time Range: January 1, 2013 – December 31, 2016
- Language: English
- Posts retrieved: 155

**Critical Window:** July 2013 (Action Plan release) – October 2015 (Final Reports + Explanatory Statement)

**Purpose:** Capture the emergence and consolidation of BEPS 1.0 discourse and its digital engagement during early international tax reform.

---

### Search 3: TCJA (U.S. Tax Reform 2017)

**Query:**
```
#TCJA | #TaxCutsAndJobsAct | #GOPTaxBill | #RepublicanTaxPlan | #TrumpTaxCuts
```

**Parameters:**
- Time Range: July 1, 2017 – October 31, 2025
- Language: English
- Posts retrieved: 1,584

**Critical Window:** November – December 2017 (legislative debate and passage)

**Purpose:** Capture the evolution of public discussion around the US Tax Cuts and Jobs Act from legislative surge to implementation, critique, and possible reactivation in subsequent policy debates.

### Search 4: Tariffs Phase 1 (Trump 1.0, 2018–2020)

**Query:**
```
#TrumpTariffs | #ChinaTariffs | #SteelTariffs | #AluminumTariffs | #TradeWar | 
#Section301 | #Section232
```

**Parameters:**
- Time Range: January 1, 2018 – December 31, 2020
- Language: English
- Posts retrieved: 4,777

**Critical Window:** March – April 2018 (initial announcement and policy implementation)

**Purpose:** Track how trade and tariff discussions unfolded during the 2018–2020 period, capturing the emergence of sustained public attention and coordinated framing of the US–China trade conflict.

### Search 5: BEPS 2.0 / Pillar Two

**Query:**
```
#PillarTwo | #PillarOne | #GlobalMinimumTax | #BEPS2 | #BEPS20 | #OECDTax | 
#G7Tax | #G20Tax | #GlobalTaxDeal | #TaxDeal | #UTPR | #IIR | #QDTT | #QDMTT | 
#GloBE | #SideBySideSolution
```

**Parameters:**
- Time Range: January 1, 2019 – October 31, 2025
- Language: English
- Posts retrieved: 266

**Critical Windows:**
- May – October 2021 (G7/G20 agreements: G7 Cornwall Summit, G20 Venice Communiqué)
- June – August 2025 (G7 shared understanding / US carve-out renegotiation)

**Purpose:** Track the full communicative life of Pillar Two—from negotiation and 2021 political announcement to the 2025 side-by-side adjustment—observing shifts between expert, political, and media framing.

---

### Search 6: Tariffs Phase 2 (Biden + Trump 2.0)

**Query:**
```
#BidenTariffs | #TrumpTariffs | #UniversalTariffs | #ReciprocalTariffs | 
#EVTariffs | #ChinaTrade | #TariffThreat
```

**Parameters:**
- Time Range: January 1, 2021 – October 31, 2025
- Language: English
- Posts retrieved: 12,641

**Critical Windows:**
- June 2022: USTR review of Section 301 tariffs and early Biden extensions
- May – August 2024: Trump 2.0 tariff escalation and campaign framing ("universal tariff," "EV tariffs")

**Purpose:** Trace the persistence and evolution of tariff discourses under Biden and Trump 2.0, capturing their ongoing digital visibility.
